How to Obtain a Non-Objection Certificate?

For 2nd year

Email the Head of the Department, requesting him/her to issue a NOC so that the student can apply for Internships and Jobs. The HOD will email the NOC back to the student.

For 3rd year

Apply for NOC at the Training and Placement Office, NIT Hamirpur.

Statement of Purpose (SOP)

The Purpose of the SOP

  • a detailed discussion of why the candidate chose the particular program
  • what he/she intends to do while pursuing the program
  • what he/she will do after having gained the education

What the SOP is Not

  • A detailed curriculum vitae
  • A boastful description of what you have achieved in your life

Key Ideas of SOP

  • Show, don't tell. Reinforce every claim you make with supporting evidence from your own experiences. Demonstrate evidence of critical thinking, and discussion of prior relevant research or project experience.
  • Motivation is great. Don't just list out your research projects as successive bullet points. What motivated you to undertake those projects? What did you learn from them?
